NTACHI-OSA RICE RECIPE

This is a simple recipe for making delicious rice.


Recipe Information

  • Servings: 4
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Difficulty: Easy

    • Ingredients

    • 2 cups of rice
    • 4 cups of water
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 4 medium fresh tomatoes (about 350g)
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
    • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
    • 1 teaspoon ground garlic
    • 1 teaspoon ground thyme
    • 1 teaspoon ground curry
    • 2 red bell peppers (large)
    • 1 large onion

    Instructions

    1. Wash the rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear.
    2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
    3. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
    4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
    5. Stir in the tomato paste, salt, pepper, ginger, garlic, thyme, and curry.
    6. Add the rice and stir to coat it with the mixture.
    7. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.
    8. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
    9. Remove from heat and let it sit covered for 5 minutes before serving.
    10. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.

    Notes

    This recipe can be customized by adding vegetables or proteins of your choice.

    Feel free to adjust the spices according to your taste preferences.

    Enjoy your delicious NTACHI-OSA rice!
